Bright Red Reason To Come Out With New EP!

Wednesday, May 11, 2011 | B

Hey all you lovely Ladies and Gents! In case some of you have been asleep for a bit or behind on your BRR news, Bright Red Reason is going to be coming out with a new EP sometime this summer! At the end of last month, Taylor, Laura and Catarina headed into the studio at The Office Recording with their good friend Mike Moschetto. So far, the demos sound awesome and the band is super excited to get this EP out to all of you. They are just putting the final touches on the EP and hope to release it in the next month or so. Check back here for all your BRR News!

Bright Red Reason Opening For The Touring On Fires!

Sunday, March 20, 2011 | B

Hey all! BRR is happy to announce that they will be opening for the currently touring Australian band The On Fires at their gig at The Democracy Center in Cambridge on Thursday April 9th! BRR will also be playing again with Speaker for the Dead and for the first time with Ticklebomb Orchestra. The show starts at 9PM and tickets are only $5! The Democracy Center is just a few blocks away from Harvard Square stop on the Red Line. Come on out and support Bright Red Reason!
